MFC California is a vibrant soccer club rooted in California, committed to bringing together and enhancing the well-being of the Indo-American community through the love of the game. Established with the vision of blending cultural heritage with athletic passion, we create a space where tradition and sportsmanship thrive. Our club fosters a sense of unity and pride, offering a platform for players of all ages and skill levels to grow, compete, and excel. Scroll down to join the club!!!
